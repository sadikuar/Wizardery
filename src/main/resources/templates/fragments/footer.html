Information

Wizardery is a webapp build for people who like table RPGs and for those who share common tastes. The aim is to let users create their RPGs and scenarios through our website so that others can see and play the said games. With functionalities such as Markdown support and file uploads, it lets you create your RPG experience in a single and common place so that other may share similar experience.
